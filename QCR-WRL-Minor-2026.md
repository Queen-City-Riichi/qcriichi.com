# QCR WRL Minor 2026

Queen City Riichi is hosting a **World Riichi League (WRL) Minor** tournament on June 14th! Participants will play with the unmodified *World Riichi Championship (WRC) 2025* ruleset. Besides physical prizes, participants will earn points toward a chance to play in the WRC 2028 (details below)! Participants may use provided reference sheets for [a full list of yaku](https://static1.squarespace.com/static/634a7884c297a25f06589b79/t/63c82bff2245392703c3a3cb/1674062885858/WRC_Yaku_1_5_3.pdf) & [a scoring guide](/membership#score-table-card), but should understand the core rules including *furiten*.

- **Format**:
  - 4-hanchan with pre-assigned seating.
  - Each hanchan lasts "75 min + 1 hand".
  - players are ranked by their cumulative outcome over 4 hanchan, for eligibility of the physical prizes.
- **Time**: 9:30 AM - 6:00 PM for the tournament. Free play until 11:30 PM.
- **Date**: June 14, 2026 (Sunday)
- **Location**: Hampton Inn & Suites Cincinnati-Mason: [5232 Bardes Rd, Mason, OH 45040](https://maps.app.goo.gl/iSAcr6D4hzmq1ZEu6)
  - Tournament takes place in the hotel's conference room, close to the lobby/breakfast area.
  - Parking is free.
- **Capacity**: 32 players max.
- **Entry fee**: $30.
- **Registration**: TBA.

## Prize Structure

Top 4 players will each choose 1 prize from the prize pool, in the order of final placements.

The prize pool is as follows (1 each):
- Amos Riichi Mahjong Portable Set
- QCR Chip Set (52 chips)
- Amos Colored Tenbou Set
- Amos Compass
